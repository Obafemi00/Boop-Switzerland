About Us

B.O.O.P Switzerland is a global media powerhouse founded by award-winning journalist, producer, speaker, and TV personality Ojinika Obiekwe.

With divisions in the United States and Nigeria, B.O.O.P—short for By Ojinika Obiekwe Productions—is more than just a media company. We are a force for storytelling, innovation, and impact.

Our mission? To entertain, inspire, and elevate voices that deserve to be heard. Through cutting-edge productions, strategic collaborations, and a deep commitment to diversity, we're not just creating content—we're rewriting the script in global media.

At B.O.O.P, we push boundaries, break barriers, and tell stories that matter—all while making a lasting social and cultural impact.

Our Founder

Ojinika Obiekwe

Emmy Award-Winning Journalist | TV Host | Producer | Speaker

Oprah Winfrey. Tyler Perry. Julia Roberts. Robert De Niro. Denzel Washington. Tom Cruise. The Rock. The Duke and Duchess of Sussex. For over two decades, Ojinika Obiekwe has been in the room with some of the world's biggest names—interviewing, reporting, and telling their stories like no one else can.

A bonafide "celebrity whisperer," Ojinika—known affectionately as Oji or "The Ojinika" on social media—started her career at the multi-Emmy Award-winning WPIX-TV in New York City, where she spent over 20 years cracking up, stumping, and connecting with A-list celebrities, politicians, and newsmakers.
