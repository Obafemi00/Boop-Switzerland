Who We Are

B.O.O.P Switzerland is a global media powerhouse founded by award-winning journalist, producer, speaker, and TV personality Ojinika Obiekwe.

With divisions in the United States and Nigeria, B.O.O.P—short for By Ojinika Obiekwe Productions—is more than just a media company. We are a force for storytelling, innovation, and impact.
